Computer Science Career Selector

July 2021 - August 2021

A webpage that offers information on careers in computer science by determining the best fit for you through your personality.

Link to project

Computer systems and mobile devices have rapidly integrated their way into our everday life and with all of these advances in technology comes a greater need for professionals in this field of study. Due to the numerous subsections and and career paths it can be very intimidated for aspiring computer professionals to find their correct niche.

With this website a user will take a brief quiz that will determine what career in computer science would best fit them based on their personality.

I used the Flask framework in order to build this web application, and Python to receive the user input and export the user's ideal computer science career.
